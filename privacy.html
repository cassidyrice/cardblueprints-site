Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 18, 2026

Your privacy matters. This policy explains plainly what information we collect on cardblueprints.com, how it is used, and who (if anyone) we share it with. We keep things simple and never sell your data — full stop.

What We Collect

  • Email address — required to deliver course lessons, newsletters, and product links.
  • Name — optional, used to personalize communications.
  • Birthday — collected for cardology readings (month and day are sufficient for most readings; year may be requested for greater precision).
  • Questions you submit — the specific question you provide with a personal reading order.
  • Payment information — processed entirely by Stripe. We never see or store your card number, CVV, or full payment details.

How We Use Your Information

  • Delivering your free email course lessons in sequence.
  • Sending occasional newsletters with new content, offers, or updates.
  • Fulfilling your personal cardology reading (recording and sending your video).
  • Sending digital product download links after purchase.
  • Responding to your questions or support requests.

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond what is listed above.

Third-Party Services

We work with a small number of trusted services to operate cardblueprints.com:

  • Stripe — Secure payment processing. Your payment data is governed by Stripe's privacy policy.
  • Buttondown — Email list management and delivery. Your email address is stored there to facilitate course delivery and newsletters.
  • Supabase — Backend database and storage. Reading order details and submitted questions are stored securely here.

Each of these providers has their own privacy policy and data practices. We do not sell or share your data with any other parties.

Email List & Unsubscribing

Every email we send includes an unsubscribe link at the bottom. You can opt out at any time, instantly and without hassle. We use Buttondown to manage our list, so unsubscribing removes you from all future mailings from Card Blueprints.

Data Retention

We keep your information for as long as you remain a subscriber or customer. If you unsubscribe or request deletion of your data, we will remove your records from our systems within a reasonable timeframe (typically within 30 days).

We Never Sell Your Data

Your personal information is never sold, rented, or traded to any third party — for any reason, ever. Full stop.

A Note on Readings

Cardology readings offered on this site are intended for entertainment and personal self-reflection purposes only. They are not a substitute for professional medical, psychological, financial, or legal advice. Please use them accordingly.
