The system

How a birth date becomes a monthly blueprint.

Every day of the year maps to a card. From that single assignment, a sequence of mathematical operations produces your complete layout.

1

Birth card assignment

Each of the 365 days maps to one of the 52 cards. Your birthday determines your birth card — a fixed position in the structure that never changes.

date(MM/DD) → card[n] where n ∈ {1..52}
2

Yearly rotation

Each year of your life, a new card takes the primary position in your layout. The sequence is determined by your birth card and current age.

card[n] + age → yearly_position
3

The 52-day cycle

Within each year, your active cards shift every 52 days. Seven periods per year, each governed by a different card. This is where month-to-month changes come from.

year_day ÷ 52 → period[0..6] → active_cards[]
4

The full layout

Birth card, yearly cards, and current period cards combine into a complete layout. Each position — long range, result, environment, displacement — describes a different dimension of the current period.

layout = { birth, yearly[7], period[active] }
